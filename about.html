Who I Am

Key points about my background and qualifications:

  • Bachelor's in Fashion Design (Sonoburg University, 2010)
  • Master's in International Marketing Management (Denmark, 2008)
  • Certified NLP Practitioner (Mumbai, 2017)
  • Master Course in Scientology (completed in 2019)

Experience & Impact

Over 15 years, I've traveled to 35+ countries to learn, teach, and inspire. My programs and workshops have helped thousands of people overcome anxiety, fear, trauma, and negative beliefs.

I also practice Aghor spirituality and study Neuroscience and alternative medicine on my own. I integrate Eastern and Western philosophies with psychological methods to support holistic well-being.

I was also involved in relief efforts after Nepal's 2015 earthquake and continue to support marginalized communities.
